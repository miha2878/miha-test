Individual and Family Services.

We aim to create strong community members and families by providing a diverse range of support services that are tailored to individual needs.

Our Individual and Family Support Services provide a diverse range of support to families with children and youth (birth to 18 years) and individuals living in the South East Metropolitan Corridor.

We respond to our community’s identified needs to ensure that our local individuals, families and their children have access to, and can receive the information, practical skills and advocacy that they need.

About Us
We aim to create community inclusion and community safety by working collaboratively with individuals, families and communities to facilitate social and economic inclusion for all and establish and sustain safe and healthy communities.

Established in 1977, we have developed into a dynamic for-purpose organisation with an enduring reputation as a community organisation that genuinely cares.

Over the years, we have distinguished ourselves as a provider of innovative services and an advocate of social and economic inclusion.

We have significant expertise in providing high quality services in Western Australia that seek to empower children, young people, individuals and families to fully participate in their communities.

Our organisation is supported by a workforce of passionate staff and volunteers. Our alliances with government, non-government and corporate partners has enabled us to make a difference in people's lives.

For over 40 years we have been Creating Futures for our most vulnerable citizens.
