About Us

Over the years of successful work, our company has become an expert in the field of laundry and dry cleaning. A wide range of services provided, a high-quality level of service, professional experience of our employees and the use of modern technologies allow us to work with people at a high professional level.

Today we are: dry cleaning of all types of clothing and textiles, as well as high-quality washing using German-made equipment, aqua cleaning - cleaning in an aquatic environment, express cleaning, cleaning, toning, water-repelling sheepskin coats, leather and suede products, cleaning fur products , individual washing of linen and shirts.

An individual approach to each client, high quality of services provided, a professional team of employees allowed us to win the trust of the most demanding clients.

Our services

  • dry cleaning of clothes made of fabrics containing natural, synthetic and artificial fibers
  • dry cleaning of products made of natural fur, leather, suede
  • dry cleaning of jerseys
  • dry cleaning workwear
  • decating of fabrics

The main types of dry cleaning carried out

  • dry cleaning of all types of clothing, wedding, evening dresses, dry cleaning of workwear
  • dry cleaning of blankets, bedspreads, blankets, blinds, curtains, soft toys
  • water cleaning of delicate products
  • dry cleaning of sheepskin coats and genuine leather products with restoration of color and any type of coatings
  • cleaning of padding polyester and feather pillows, with disinfection and restoration
