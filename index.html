Commodity control and expert assessments

  • Commodity inspections

    Control of quantity and quality during preliminary storage of goods in port warehouses and warehouse bases, inspection of goods in order to establish the type and condition of goods in accordance with the import / export trade agreement.

    Control during loading / unloading of vehicles, quantity control (by weighing on scales, draft survey, street survey, counting).

  • Insurance claims

    As Lloyd's agent for Bulgaria, Fidelitas serves the international insurance market, offering a full range of services related to the assessment of insurance losses and the settlement of insurance claims, including:

    Insurance inspections and assessments - establishing the nature, size and cause of damage to all types of goods transported by sea, air or land, as a result of a private or general accident.

  • Property inspections

    Inspection and assessment of damage to buildings, machinery, structures and material supplies as a result of floods, fires, natural disasters and other risks

    Risk assessment in property / inventory insurance
