Roadmap

  • Stage 1

    Players can participate by buying one or more cards on the AtomicHub, buying card sets, creating the cards to prepare for the gameplay, and buying land cards where players can upgrade cities and receive cryptocurrency for this, and their owners can share their profits.

  • Stage 2

    The release of the demo version of the game. Launching an interactive land map. The output of the gaming cryptocurrency "NewOrigin" (NON) and its staking. You can farm cryptocurrency using nft.

  • Stage 3

    The output of the cryptocurrency to the Defi - exchange. Launching a full version game. Token NON, limited by the time of land restoration. The recovery progress will be displayed on the site.
