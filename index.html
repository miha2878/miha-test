Cratf cards

Wood, brick and iron are simple consumables, they are needed to create a village, town or city. For one land you need four identical consumables and glass. The amount of glass is limited to three hundred units and will not be re-produced, it is only needed to create lands. While wood, brick and iron will be produced repeatedly. They will be used to build various items that can be placed on your own or someone else's land, they will have different characteristics and increase income.
