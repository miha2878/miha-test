About Us

Outsourcing includes a full range of services in the field of personnel search and selection, outsourcing of administrative processes, business support and contractor management.

We conduct comprehensive audits of business processes, develop and implement unique solutions.

We are ready to take on such a small area as replacing the secretary at the reception for 1 hour, as well as such work as, for example, digitizing the accounting archive or even repairing a meeting room.

Our solve

We solve the problems of non-core administrative functions for accounting, AXO, marketing, sales, procurement, personnel departments. But we are not limited to these works:

  • recruitment of secretaries and assistants who are trained at the Institute of Secretaries Office line
  • organization of the reception area (call center): guest reception, office reception, call reception
  • management of the service of working with documents: printing and scanning, archiving and storage, organization and maintenance of document circulation.
  • office inventory, installation work that does not require high qualifications
  • supplier management: courier services, cleaning, office maintenance and operation, catering, office purchases (water, stationery, etc.), taxi services, travel (organization of business trips), medical services and insurance, mobile communications
This is a list of our standard services, while we can help with other jobs where you have a lack of resources. The presence of a base of executors helps us to provide service continuously, regardless of the time of the day or the shift of the employee.
