About Us

Our mission is to be the best in shaping the modern human environment. Constantly develop and embody the brightest ideas and dreams of comfort in reality. Make the world a better place and make people - customers, employees, and others - happier. Create a dream space with DRAPERY!

We are pleased to offer you a full range of home furniture: living rooms, bedrooms, libraries, offices, kitchens, furniture for children's rooms, hallways, bathrooms, upholstered furniture and mattresses. We produce furniture in various styles: from classic to modern; all kinds of color solutions and price ranges.In a number of regions, our company offers custom-made furniture. We are also ready to fulfill your order for the manufacture, assembly and installation of furniture for government agencies, educational institutions, shopping centers and entertainment complexes.
