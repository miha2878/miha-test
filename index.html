About us

We conduct accident analysis to determine the cause or causes of the accident (which may lead to one or more outcomes) in order to prevent further accidents of this kind. This is part of the investigation of aircraft accidents or incidents. These analyzes can be performed by a variety of experts, including forensic experts, forensic engineers, or health and safety consultants.

Working for more than 10 years, we try to reduce the number of accidents in the world, improve the safety of workplaces. A team of more than 100 people, not counting the involved volunteers, monitor the health of roads, workplaces, service areas, as well as just parks and recreation areas. Indicate problem areas in shopping malls, entertainment centers and areas for children.
